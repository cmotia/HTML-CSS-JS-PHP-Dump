Problem Statement

An annual music festival with artists, performers and bands, wants to hold a give away with various items. The items range from free tickets, meet and greets to t-shirts.

Solution

When given this assignment we had to think of an idea on how to manage all this into one system. As a group we had decided to use a method similar to what other music festivals have used before. RFID wristbands are the solution to our problems at music festivals. These wrist bands have everything built into them. Tracking, cashless payments,medical information, easy entrance into the festivals and much more. One example being t-shirt size so that only contestants the correct size will be entered in a raffle for a shirt. How do these wristbands work? They have a small computer chip built into them which can manage all these systems. These computer chips are connected to a database which holds everyone's information. These RFID chips have backup systems just in case the power where to go out at the festival, people would still be able to get in.


You might think all these scanners would slow down traffic but if every entrance has 8 scanners (each costing around 1500)like you would see at an amusement park and each stage has around 6 entrances and assuming it will take a person around 10 seconds to get in through the scanners thats 48 people who can get in every 10 seconds so within 17 minutes we could get approximately 4,800 people into a stage. Bear in mind this is a low end estimate as for all practical purposes scanning in shouldnt take more than 5 seconds.

Our solution meets the problem requirement for the project because when you enter a stage area you would register for the raffle for that performance. These RFID chips have a tracking system in them so once you entered the geofence you would be automatically entered into the raffle.

Marketing

RFID scanners cost around $1000-$2000 a scanner. You would have these scanners at every entrance and exit as well it takes anywhere from 3-5 seconds to scan a wristband allowing you to scan thousands of people fast. Companies are going to want this technology becuase it makes the overall festival run a lot more efficiently and smoothly.

The people who we are trying to sell our product to would be event organizers and festival planners, but I believe this technology can be used for more then that, I believe you could use this systems in large factories where you can keep track of your workers as all times.
